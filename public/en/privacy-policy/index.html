Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 20, 2026

Overview

bestcityinchina.site ("the site") is a free travel quiz tool that helps travelers choose Chinese cities to visit. We take your privacy seriously and collect minimal data.

What data we collect

We do not collect any personally identifiable information (PII). We do not require accounts, registration, or email addresses. Quiz answers are processed entirely in your browser and are never sent to our servers.

We use Google Analytics 4 (GA4) to collect anonymous usage data, including:

  • Pages visited and time spent on each page
  • General geographic region (country/region level only)
  • Browser type and device category
  • Referral source (how you found the site)
  • Quiz interaction events (start, completion, result viewed)

This data is aggregated and anonymous. It cannot be used to identify individual users.

Cookies

Google Analytics sets cookies to distinguish unique sessions. These are first-party analytics cookies. We do not use advertising cookies, tracking pixels, or third-party marketing cookies.

You can opt out of Google Analytics tracking by installing the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

Third-party services

The site uses the following third-party services:

  • Google Analytics 4 — anonymous usage analytics (Google Privacy Policy)
  • Google Fonts — web font delivery (no personal data stored)
  • Netlify — website hosting (standard server logs)

Data retention

Google Analytics data is retained for 14 months. We do not store any other user data.

Your rights

Since we do not collect personal data, there is no personal information to access, correct, or delete. If you have questions about analytics data collected by Google, refer to Google's Privacy Policy.

Children's privacy

This site is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy occasionally. The "last updated" date at the top of this page reflects the most recent revision.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, email team@bestcityinchina.site or visit our contact page.
